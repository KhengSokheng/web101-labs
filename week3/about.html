About Us

The Story behind Acme Co.

Our Story

Acme Co. begin in 2015 as a small workshop with one simple idea make thing people will actually keep. Our founder grew tired of disposable and wanted to bring baack the quality of an earlier era - when thing were build to last and made by hand that cared.

Over a decade later. we've grown into the team of designers, makers, and customer, but the original ideal hasn't changed. We still make every products as if we were going to use it ourselves.

Our mission

To craft everyday goods that earn their place in your life - through quality materials, honest design, and the kind of finish you can feel in your hands.

Our Values

  • 1.Craft - We design slowly and build carefully. Shortcuts cost more tahn they save.
  • 2.Honesty - We tell you what something is made of , where it came from and what it costs us to make.
  • 3.longevity - Products should outline trends. Repairs should be possible. Disposable is not a value.
  • 4.People - real customer, real maker, real conversations. We answer every email ourselves.
